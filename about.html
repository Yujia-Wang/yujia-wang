About Me


My name is Yujia (Bobo) Wang. Currently I'm studying my master's degree in HCI at Georgia Tech. As an engineer, I'm passionate about developing interactive technologies to offer people best user experiences.

And I'm also a designer. I love designing UI with intriguing interactions. My design process is always evidence-based and user-centered because I believe the ultimate purpose of design is to serve user. To achieve this purpose, feedback from users is crucial.

My background is automation, which focuses on both hardware and software programming. I have been working on interactive technology since an undergrad. My research interests include front-end technology, wearable technology, AR/VR. These emerging technologies are fascinating, but I hope people would not only use them as tools, but also enjoy this whole process when they use them.

I'm a quick learner, and always curious about everything. I'm not afraid of challenges, and always growing up as conquering challenges.

This is my first year studying at Georgia Tech, and now I'm seeking UX Engineer Internship for Summer 2018.
